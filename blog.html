My Story


At age 17 I had few issues which lead me into drepression and loss of confidence. It was so severe that i wanted to stop my education forever. I had no serious goals set for the future. I was wage on what I wanted to do with my life. And this was not onlt panicking me up but also taking my drepression into a severe condition. I still remember that day when i locked myself up in a room. Not wanting to see people nor wanting speak to them. My Parents were petrifed as well.


My mum then started taking me to yoga classes. I was reluctant, like anyother 17year old I reffused to go. A week passed and then another. I slowly realized yoga and meditation were helping me cope up with my situation, I was better, my thoughts were more clear, I realized its okay not to have future thoughts or goals but its better though. Yoga gave me the power to see life from a different perpective and challenge things that come up rather than shutting them down and ignoring to face them. I got back a pro version of myself, much more happier, confident and stronger.









My Passion


My Passion derives from my story. I did yoga continuously for 6 months then and saw a huge positive change both physically and mentally. I have taken "Bachelor of science in yoga" as a distance education apart from my "Bachelor of Computer science". I graduated from both successfully and hence I have decided to make yoga as my passion (thats one of the things that I would love to do) into small partime that would help me as well as others grow and help them get through some tough times. I thought yoga to many children and adults untill I came to pursue my master abroad.
