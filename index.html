What is yoga?



Yoga is the perfect control of mind and consciousness at a level with perfection in it. It is universal, no bound by dogmas and beliefs of any particular religion. Thus it is available to all irrespective of any limitations such as sex, natinality, age, etc. Yoga is a complete freedom from misery. Yoga is very old, well-tested scientific system of training human organisum in all its aspects-body, mind and sprit to bring into the highest possible integration.

Yoga is well-known to touch and influence all the aspects of human life and activity. The practice of yoga prevents psychosomatic disorders/diseases and improves and individuals resistance and ability to endure stressful situations. Yoga is a very ancient science. It is thousands of years old. The perceptions derived from its practice form the backbone of the greatness of India, Which for centuries has been legendary. The truths espoused in the yoga teachings, however, are not limited to India, nor to those who consciously practice yoga techniques.
