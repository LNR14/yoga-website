This Page is photo gallery which consists of 12 major yoga poses. Every pose is labelled with its name, level and benifits.

YOGA   POSES   GALLERY

HANUMANASANA

Level: Intermediate

Benifits: known as monkey pose helps stretchs hamstrings, groin muscles, and hip flexor.

PADMASANA

Level: Intermediate

Benifits: known as Lotus pose helps keep the brain calm, develops good posture and restores energy levels.

VRIKSHASANA

Level: Beginner

Benifits: known as Tree Pose helps stretches the thighs, groins, torso, and shoulders. Heals flat feet and is therapeutic for sciatica.

NATARAJASANA

Level: Intermediate

Benifits: Known as Dance pose helps hips get toned and chest is expand to make space for the breath.

VIRABHADRASANA I

Level: Beginner

Benifits: known as the Warrior Pose 1 which helps strengthen thigh, back, calf, ankle, shoulder and arm

VIRABHADRASANA II

Level: Intermediate

Benifits: known as the Warrior Pose 2, it enhances strength, stability, and concentration and Stretches Lung, Ankle, Shoulder, Leg

VIRABHADRASANA III

Level: Advanced

Benifits: known as the Warrior Pose, it challenges your body with both backward- and forward-bending elements.

TRIKONASANA

Level: Intermediate

Benifits: known as Triangle pose it strengthens Knee, Thigh, Ankle and Stretches Knee, Vertebral column, Ankle, Shoulder.

TITTIBHASANA

Level: Advanced

Benifits: known as firefly or insect pose helps strengthens Wrist, Arm and stretches inner groins, back torso.

VRISCHIKASANA

Level: Advanced

Benifits: known as scorpion pose, it improves circulation in the lower limbs and abdomen. Tones the reproductive organs.

USTRASANA I

Level: Advanced

Benifits: known as camel pose, it increases circulation of blood, detoxces the body while increasing blood flow to your face.

SHIRSHASANA

Level: Advanced

Benifits: known as Yoga Headstand, helps increases the blood circulation in the brain and upper part of the body.
